A COURT OF THRONS AND ROSES

She stole a life

Now she must pay with her heart

Synopsis

When nineteen-year-old huntress Feyre kills a wolf in the woods, a terrifying creature arrives to demand retribution.Dragged to a treacherous magical land she knows about only from legends, Feyre discovers that her captor is not truly a beast, but one of the lethal, immortal faeries who once ruled her world. AS Feyre dwells on his estate, her feeling for Tamlin transform from icy hostility into fiery passion that burns through every lie and warning. She is been told about the beautiful, dangerous world of Fae. But an ancient, wicked shadow over faerie land is growing and Feyre must find a way to stop it… or doom Tamlin and his world forever...

Novel by Sarah J.Maas
